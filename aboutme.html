Hello!

I would like to thank you for taking the time to Visit my website.

I started coding on a TI-82 as a kid and have been coding since the 1990's.

When I learned AutoCAD, my passion to create grew in the field of design. Coding and design grew to be a hobby, in my spare time for the personal challenge. I have earned certifications throughout the years, have taken college classes, and  have a longstanding knowledge in coding, design, and script. In college I learned XHTML, at the time it was how you created CSS. I know old school. But have kept up and recently updated my certificates to HTML5 and CSS3 (since these are the industry standard).   While I have learned and worked with both Python and JavaScript throughout the years, I am currently in the process of acquiring certifications for both, beyond a self taught knowledge. I believe that my personal use and the training/certificates show a deep sub code running in my veins.  For my own mental calisthenics I have been programming my own game in my free time and have enjoyed dabbling in photogrammetry.  My passion is and has been designing.  I would welcome the opportunity to prove my passion: In well thought-out and properly executed designs!  I believe with the right tools and my strong work ethic, together we can create!
